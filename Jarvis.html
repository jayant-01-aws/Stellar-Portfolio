Jarvis & Eye Mouse Detector

A sophisticated automation ecosystem that blends voice-activated AI with hands-free peripheral control. Using MediaPipe's facial landmark detection, the system maps ocular movements to mouse coordinates, allowing users to navigate their OS through natural gaze.

Python OpenCV MediaPipe PyAutoGUI NLP

Computer Vision

Utilizes the Iris Solution to track 468 facial landmarks, calculating the specific iris centroid for 1:1 screen mapping.

Voice Interface

Integrated with Speech Recognition and pyttsx3 to provide a seamless, Iron Man-inspired conversational experience.

System Hooks

Employs PyAutoGUI for low-level hardware control, enabling clicks via eye-blink detection (aspect ratio calculation).

Multithreading

Runs the CV engine and the Voice engine on separate threads to ensure zero lag in mouse movement during speech.
